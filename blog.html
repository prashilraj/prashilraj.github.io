Green Screen

Sometimes I wish my life to be the green screen as in the cinemas that can be tweaked into whatever I want. I am done with this world and pleasing everybody around me. So I want something that could take my place and people see whatever they wanted to see within me. Cause original me is not someone you wants to be around, you dont want all those sadness to crawl into you and weakens you. You want what you think you wanted from me. So let me be a green screen for you. And see all the things you desired in me, within me, i wanted to be your world so what's better than a green screen ? You are thinking me as a fake but how could i be a fake if i am already telling you that the real me for you would be a fake ? I think fake is someone who keep on lying to you but here i am already telling you the truth. So let be your world and give what you wanted from me. I am all yours to keep so hold me tight and let me take care of you. Give me all your scars and take what you wanted from me: as i am a green screen for you. .
